Nintendo Wii

The Nintendo Wii is one of my favorite consoles of all time. I love it so much because of the nostalgia and the amazing vibes the menu and games give. The relaxing menu music, the Mii Channel, and all those fun motion games really made the Wii an epic console.

Titles like Wii Sports, Mario Kart Wii, and Super Mario Galaxy bring back awesome memories. Just turning it on and playing those games makes me feel cozy and happy :D.

Wii Shop Channel

The Wii Shop Channel was legendary. The music? Arguably top 5 most iconic things of all time. I loved browsing through Virtual Console games and WiiWare titles. You could grab old-school classics and explore new hidden gems.

Even though the Wii Shop is gone now, you can access the Wii Shop Channel on your again with Wiimart!
